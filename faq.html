Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?
We proudly serve all of West Virginia and Pennsylvania, including Gerrardstown, Martinsburg, Inwood, Charles Town, Shepherdstown, Berkeley Springs, and surrounding communities.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes. A+ Paving & Landscaping is fully licensed (WV0585) and insured, giving you complete peace of mind on every project.
Do you offer free estimates?
Absolutely. We provide free, no-obligation estimates for all of our services. Call us at (304) 579-3872 or use our online contact form to get started.
What paving services do you offer?
We offer a full range of paving services including asphalt driveways, parking lots, asphalt overlays, tar & chip, driveway extensions, sealcoating, grading, excavation, and road repair. Visit our services page for the full list.
How long does a typical driveway paving project take?
Most residential driveway projects are completed in 1 to 3 days, depending on the size and scope of the work. We'll give you a clear timeline during your free estimate.
What is the difference between asphalt and tar & chip?
Asphalt provides a smooth, uniform surface ideal for driveways and parking lots. Tar & chip offers a more textured, rustic look at a lower cost and is popular for rural driveways and country roads. Both are durable options — we can help you choose the best fit during your estimate.
How often should I sealcoat my driveway?
We recommend sealcoating your asphalt driveway every 2 to 3 years. Regular sealcoating protects against weather damage, UV rays, and oil stains, extending the life of your pavement significantly.
Do you handle landscaping and hardscaping too?
Yes! In addition to paving, we offer landscaping services including garden bed design, plantings, concrete patio installation, and porch makeovers. Check out our gallery to see examples of our work.

We accept cash, checks, and major credit cards. Payment details and scheduling are discussed during your estimate so there are no surprises.
