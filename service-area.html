Our Service Area

A+ Paving & Landscaping proudly serves communities throughout West Virginia and Pennsylvania. From residential driveways to commercial parking lots, we bring quality craftsmanship to your doorstep.

West Virginia

Paving in Martinsburg, WV

As the county seat of Berkeley County, Martinsburg is one of our most-served areas. We handle driveways, parking lots, sealcoating, and road repair throughout the city and surrounding neighborhoods.

Driveway Contractors in Inwood, WV

Inwood homeowners and businesses trust A+ Paving for reliable asphalt work, tar & chip driveways, and landscaping services. We're just a short drive away.

Paving in Gerrardstown, WV

Our home base. Gerrardstown is where it all started, and we take pride in serving our neighbors with top-quality paving and landscaping.

Paving in Charles Town, WV

From historic properties to new developments, we provide expert paving and hardscaping services throughout Charles Town and Jefferson County.

Paving in Shepherdstown, WV

West Virginia's oldest town deserves the best. We serve Shepherdstown with driveways, patios, landscaping, and more.

Paving in Berkeley Springs, WV

Morgan County residents count on A+ Paving for driveway installation, sealcoating, grading, and excavation services.

Additional WV Communities

We also serve Ranson, Hedgesville, Falling Waters, Bunker Hill, Kearneysville, and communities throughout Berkeley, Jefferson, and Morgan counties.
